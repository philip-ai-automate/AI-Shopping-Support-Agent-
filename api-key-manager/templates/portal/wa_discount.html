How WhatsApp Discounts Work

Merchant Only: AI never offers a discount. Any discount request → immediate handoff to you.

AI Then Merchant: AI offers your configured discount automatically. If the customer wants more → escalated to you.

Customers trigger discounts by replying DISCOUNT during the ordering flow.
