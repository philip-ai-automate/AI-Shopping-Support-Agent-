📋 Client Requirements Checklist
What a business must have before they can go live with PhiXtra AI WhatsApp.
  1. Get a brand new SIM card or a number that has never been used on WhatsApp. This number is just for business.
  2. If it's already on WhatsApp, they must delete that WhatsApp account on the number first.
  3. Keep the SIM active — they'll need to receive a one-time code (OTP) during setup.
  4. Share the number with PhiXtra — we'll connect it to the WhatsApp Business API during onboarding.
💡 MTN, Glo, Airtel, and 9mobile SIMs all work.
  1. Go to business.facebook.com on a computer and click "Create Account".
  2. Enter the business name, owner's full name, and business email. Click Submit.
  3. Check the email inbox — click the confirmation link Meta sends.
  4. Save the login details safely — they'll need them again during setup.
  5. Find their Meta Business ID: Settings → Business Info — it's a long number at the top.
💡 They need a personal Facebook account first. It's free to create one.
  1. Log in to business.facebook.com and go to Settings → Business Assets → WhatsApp Accounts.
  2. Click "Add" and enter the business phone number.
  3. Meta will send an OTP to that number — enter it to confirm.
  4. Share the phone number and Meta Business ID with PhiXtra to complete the connection.
💡 Meta verification can take 1–2 days. This is normal — PhiXtra support will guide them through it.
