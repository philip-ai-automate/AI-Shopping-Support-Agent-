🔀 Handoff Rules
Configure when your AI automatically alerts your team that a visitor needs human help

⚡ How this works

When a rule is ON, the AI watches for that situation during every conversation. The moment it is triggered, your team gets an instant email alert with the visitor's WhatsApp number, and the conversation appears in the Needs Attention panel on your Dashboard.

Visitor-initiated rules fire when the visitor says something (e.g. "I want to speak to someone"). AI-decided rules fire silently — the visitor doesn't need to ask, the AI recognises the situation on its own (e.g. visitor asks about a promo offer).

No system prompt editing needed — changes here take effect on the very next chat.
