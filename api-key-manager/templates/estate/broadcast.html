How Broadcast Works
Works — Active contacts

Contacts who sent you a WhatsApp message within the last 24 hours are in an open session. You can send them any free-form message and it will be delivered.

Limited — Older contacts

If a contact last messaged you more than 24 hours ago, Meta requires a pre-approved template. Free-form messages will be blocked. Re-engage them when they reach out again.

Won't work — Cold contacts

Contacts you added manually or from CSV who have never WhatsApp'd you — Meta will reject the message. Best for follow-up after they've messaged you first.

This is a WhatsApp Business API rule, not a PhiXtra limitation. Meta blocks unsolicited messages to protect users from spam.
