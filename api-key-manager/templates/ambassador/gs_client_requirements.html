What to tell the shop owner

Before a shop can go live on PhiXtra, they need to have four things ready. When a shop owner says yes, share this list with them. Tap each one to see how to help them get it.

  1. Get a brand new SIM card — or any number that has never been used on WhatsApp before. This number is only for the business.
  2. If the number is already on WhatsApp, they must delete that WhatsApp account first before continuing.
  3. Keep the SIM active — they will need to get a one-time code (OTP) on that number during setup.
  4. Give the number to PhiXtra — the team will connect it to WhatsApp Business for them.
💡 MTN, Glo, Airtel, and 9mobile SIMs all work fine.
  1. Go to business.facebook.com on a computer or laptop and click "Create Account".
  2. Enter the business name, owner's name, and a business email address. Click Submit.
  3. Check that email inbox — click the confirmation link that Meta sends.
  4. Save the login details somewhere safe — they will need them again later.
  5. Find the Meta Business ID: go to Settings → Business Info — it is a long number near the top.
💡 They need a personal Facebook account to do this. It is free to create one.
  1. Log in to business.facebook.com and go to Settings → Business Assets → WhatsApp Accounts.
  2. Click "Add" and type in the shop's phone number.
  3. Meta will send a one-time code (OTP) to that number — enter it to confirm.
  4. Share the phone number and Meta Business ID with PhiXtra to finish the connection.
💡 Meta can take 1–2 days to verify. This is normal — PhiXtra support will help them through it.
  1. Open Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets (free at sheets.google.com).
  2. Add these column headings in the first row: Product Name | Price | Description | In Stock (Yes or No).
  3. Fill in one product per row — for example: iPhone 15 | ₦850,000 | Brand new, 128GB, Black | Yes.
  4. Save the file as .xlsx or .csv. In Google Sheets, go to File → Download → Microsoft Excel.
  5. Upload the file in PhiXtra under "Product Import" after signing up.
💡 The more details they add in the description, the better the AI can answer customer questions. Tell them to include things like colour, storage size, condition, and warranty.
Tip: Send this list to the shop owner on WhatsApp after they agree to sign up. The sooner they get these things ready, the sooner they go live — and the sooner you start earning.
