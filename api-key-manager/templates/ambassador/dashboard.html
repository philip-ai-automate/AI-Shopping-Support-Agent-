📋 Client Requirements Checklist

Before you pitch PhiXtra AI to a business, make sure they have all four of these things ready. Tap each one to see how to help them get it if they don't have it yet.

How to help them get it
  1. Ask the business to get a brand new SIM card or a phone number that has never been used on WhatsApp before. This number is just for business — not for personal calls or chats.
  2. If that number is already active on WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business, they must delete the WhatsApp account on that number first before moving on.
  3. Tell them to keep the SIM card active and available — they will need to receive a one-time code (OTP) on it during the setup process.
  4. Once they have the number ready, share it with PhiXtra. PhiXtra will connect it to the WhatsApp Business API during onboarding.
💡 Tip: The number must belong to the business — not a personal line. MTN, Glo, Airtel, and 9mobile SIMs all work.
How to help them get it
  1. Open a computer (a phone can work but a computer is easier) and go to business.facebook.com.
  2. Click "Create Account" at the top right of the page.
  3. Type in the business name, the owner's full name, and the business email address. Click Submit.
  4. Check that email inbox — Meta will send a confirmation email. Click the link inside that email to verify the account.
  5. The business now has a Meta Business Account. Tell them to write down the email and password somewhere safe — they will need it again.
  6. Ask them to send you their Meta Business ID — they can find it by going to business.facebook.com, clicking Settings, then Business Info. The ID is a long number at the top.
💡 Tip: They need a personal Facebook account to create a Meta Business Account. If they don't have one, they need to create a Facebook account first — it's free.
How to help them get it
  1. Log in to business.facebook.com with the Meta Business Account created in Step 2.
  2. On the left-hand side menu, click "Settings" (the gear icon), then click "Business Assets".
  3. Look for "WhatsApp Accounts" and click "Add".
  4. Enter the business phone number from Requirement 1. Make sure the SIM card is nearby — Meta will send a one-time code (OTP) to that number.
  5. Enter the OTP code when asked. The number is now linked to Meta.
  6. The business is now ready for PhiXtra to complete the connection. Share the phone number and Meta Business ID with PhiXtra support to finish the setup.
💡 Tip: This step can take 1–2 days if Meta asks for extra business verification. Reassure the business that this is normal and PhiXtra support will guide them through it.
How to help them get it
  1. Open Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets (Google Sheets is free at sheets.google.com).
  2. In the very first row, create these headings in separate columns: Product Name | Price | Description | In Stock (Yes or No).
  3. Starting from the second row, fill in one product per row. For example: iPhone 14 | ₦850,000 | Brand new, 128GB, Black | Yes.
  4. Make sure every product has a correct price and a short description. The AI uses this to answer customer questions.
  5. When all products are added, save the file as .xlsx (Excel format) or .csv. In Google Sheets, click File → Download → Microsoft Excel.
  6. This file will be uploaded to PhiXtra during their portal onboarding under "Product Import".
💡 Tip: The more detail in the description, the smarter the AI will be when answering customer questions. Encourage businesses to be as specific as possible — sizes, colours, conditions, warranties, etc.
