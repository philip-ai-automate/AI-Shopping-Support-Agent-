Other businesses you can refer

You can refer any business that uses WhatsApp to talk to customers. But if they are not a phone or computer shop, the PhiXtra team needs to set up their account first.

⏱️
48-hour setup time — For any business outside phone and computer shops, the PhiXtra team will set up their account. They will be ready within 48 hours of signing up. Always tell them this upfront: "Setup takes up to 48 hours, then you're live."
The best question to ask

Ask this when you walk into a shop. The answer will tell you right away if they need PhiXtra:

"How many WhatsApp messages do you get a day asking about prices, specs, or stock?"
If they say more than 10 — they need PhiXtra.
If they say they miss messages or reply late — they really need PhiXtra.
If they say "I can't keep up" — you have a sale.
