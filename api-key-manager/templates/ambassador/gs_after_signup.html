Why you need to follow up

If a shop signs up but never sets up their account, they will not pay a subscription — and you will not earn anything. A short follow-up from you makes a big difference.

5 things to do after they sign up
1
Check they got the welcome email
Tell them to check their spam folder if they do not see it. The email has their login link.
2
Ask them to log in at portal.phixtra.com
Once they log in, they need to connect their WhatsApp number. This is the most important step.
3
Ask them to upload their product list
The AI needs the product list to answer customer questions. Tell them to upload their spreadsheet under "Product Import".
4
Check in after 2–3 days
Ask if the AI is working well for them. If they have any problems, point them to the WhatsApp support group. Shops that are happy stay subscribed longer.
5
Encourage them to upgrade to a higher plan
Once they are happy, let them know the Growth and Pro plans unlock more features. You earn an upgrade bonus every time they move up — ₦5,000 (Starter→Growth), ₦10,000 (Growth→Pro), ₦15,000 (Starter→Pro), or ₦20,000 (Free→Pro).
Send them this on WhatsApp after they sign up
Copy & send this
Hi [Name], welcome to PhiXtra! 🎉 Here is how to get started: 1. Log in at portal.phixtra.com 2. Connect your WhatsApp number 3. Upload your product list (Excel or CSV) Once your number is connected, the AI will start replying to customer messages for you. Let me know if you need any help — I am here!
Remember: You start earning the moment they make their first payment. The faster they set up, the faster you get paid.
