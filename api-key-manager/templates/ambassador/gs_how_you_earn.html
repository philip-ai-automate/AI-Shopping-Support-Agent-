Monthly commission

You earn 20% of every monthly payment your referred shops make. This is paid automatically — you do not need to ask or send an invoice.

Level Active Shops Your Cut How Long
Standard 1 – 9 20% 12 months
Elite 10 – 19 20% 24 months
Master 🏆 20+ 20% Forever
Your level goes up as more of your referred shops become paying subscribers. The more shops you bring in, the longer you keep earning from each one.
