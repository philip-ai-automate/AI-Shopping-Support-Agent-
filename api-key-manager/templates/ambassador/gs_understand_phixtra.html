What is PhiXtra?

PhiXtra is an AI assistant that runs on a shop's WhatsApp line. When a customer sends a message — asking about a price, checking if something is in stock, or placing an order — the AI replies for the shop automatically, day and night, without the shop owner doing anything.

⚡
Replies in seconds — customers never wait. The AI answers instantly, even at 2am when the shop is closed.
📦
Knows the shop's products — the shop uploads their product list and the AI learns it. It can answer questions about prices, specs, stock, and more.
👤
Talks like a real person — it does not sound like a bot. Customers get clear, helpful replies in plain language.
📊
The shop stays in control — the owner can log in any time to see all conversations, update products, and change how the AI behaves.
🔀
Hands over to a human when needed — if a customer has a complex issue, the AI can pass the conversation to the shop owner to handle.

What to explore in the demo

Once you log in, here is what to look at — and what to point out when showing a client.

🏠
Dashboard
Shows total messages handled, active conversations, and AI performance at a glance.
Tell a client: "This is your control room. You can see everything that is happening with your customers."
💬
Inbox
All customer WhatsApp conversations in one place. The AI handles them but the shop owner can read every message and jump in if needed.
Tell a client: "You can see every chat your AI has with customers. Nothing is hidden."
📦
Products
The shop's product catalogue. This is where they upload their list — and what the AI uses to answer customer questions about prices and stock.
Tell a client: "Whatever you add here, the AI knows. Update a price and the AI will quote the new price from that moment."
📱
WhatsApp Connect
Shows the business WhatsApp number that is connected to the AI. This is the number customers message.
Tell a client: "This is where we connect your WhatsApp number. Once it is linked, the AI takes over."
💳
Plans & Billing
Shows the available plans and what each one includes. The shop picks a plan after signing up.
Tell a client: "There is a free trial to start. You only pay when you are happy with how it works."
When showing a client: Log in on your phone and hand it to them to scroll through. Let them see the inbox and products themselves. Seeing real conversations in the inbox is usually what convinces them.
