1. Appointment

PhiXtra AI hereby appoints the Ambassador as a non-exclusive independent partner to promote and refer clients to PhiXtra AI's products and services. This agreement does not create an employment relationship.

2. Commission Structure

2.1 Self-Closed Referrals
Where a business signs up directly through the Ambassador's unique referral link, the Ambassador earns 30% of the client's monthly subscription fee for the duration of their active commission window (see Clause 4).


2.2 Submitted Leads
Where the Ambassador submits a business lead via the PhiXtra Ambassador Portal and PhiXtra AI's team closes and onboards the client, the Ambassador earns 20% of the client's monthly subscription fee, beginning from the date the client activates a paid subscription.


2.3 Upsell Bonuses
The Ambassador earns a one-time bonus when a referred client upgrades to a higher plan:

4. Commission Window

Commission is earned from the Ambassador's approval date until the end of their tier's commission window. Commissions on clients referred before the window closes continue to be earned until that client churns or the window ends, whichever comes first. Master tier Ambassadors earn commissions indefinitely.

5. Payouts

Commissions are paid on the last business day of each calendar month. Minimum payout threshold: ₦5,000 — balances below this roll over to the following month. Payment is made via bank transfer to the account details provided in the Ambassador's profile. PhiXtra AI reserves the right to withhold payment for commissions under dispute or where fraudulent activity is suspected.

6. Ambassador Obligations

The Ambassador agrees to:

  • Promote PhiXtra AI honestly and accurately
  • Not make false or misleading claims about PhiXtra AI's products
  • Not approach existing PhiXtra AI clients as new referrals
  • Not use spam, unsolicited bulk messaging, or prohibited advertising methods
  • Maintain the confidentiality of any non-public information shared by PhiXtra AI

7. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 14 days' written notice to the other. On termination, no new commissions are earned after the termination date; commissions already earned and due before termination will be paid on the next regular payout date; and the Ambassador's referral link will be deactivated. PhiXtra AI may terminate immediately (without notice) for fraud, breach of this agreement, or conduct damaging to PhiXtra AI's reputation.

8. Independent Contractor

The Ambassador is an independent contractor. Nothing in this agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, agency, franchise, or employment relationship. The Ambassador is solely responsible for any applicable taxes on earnings received.

9. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Any disputes shall be resolved through good-faith negotiation, failing which through the courts of Nigeria.

10. Acceptance

The Ambassador accepted this agreement during registration and confirmed their understanding of all terms at that time.
