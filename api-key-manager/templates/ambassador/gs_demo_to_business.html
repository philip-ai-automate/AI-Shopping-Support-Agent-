Why show a demo first?

Telling a shop owner about the AI is good. Showing them the AI replying in real time is better. Most shop owners sign up on the spot once they see it working. The demo takes less than 2 minutes.

The demo WhatsApp account

This is a live PhiXtra AI account running on a real phone shop — profitbuyz.com. It knows iPhone prices, specs, and stock. You will use it to show shop owners exactly what their customers will experience.

📱
iPhones only for now. This demo account only knows about iPhones at the moment. Only ask iPhone questions during the demo. Do not type questions about Samsung, Tecno, laptops, or other products — the AI will not have those answers yet.

How to run the demo
1
Open the demo chat in front of the shop owner
Tap the green button above or open WhatsApp and search for the demo number. Do this while they are watching.
2
Type an iPhone question and send it
Use one of the sample questions below. Type it slowly so they can see what you are doing.
3
Show them the reply
The AI will reply in seconds. Hold up your phone so they can read the answer. Let it sink in.
4
Let them type their own question
Hand your phone to the shop owner and ask them to type any iPhone question they want. This is the moment that usually closes the deal.
5
Say this to close
"This is exactly what your customers will get when they message your shop. Ready to set yours up? I can sign you up right now."
Sample questions to use during the demo

Copy any of these into the demo chat. Only use iPhone questions — that is what this demo account knows.

How much is iPhone 15?
Do you have iPhone 14 Pro Max in stock?
What is the price of iPhone 13 128GB?
Is iPhone 15 Pro available in black?
What is the difference between iPhone 14 and iPhone 15?
Remember: Only ask iPhone questions during the demo. If a shop owner asks about other phones or products, tell them: "The demo only covers iPhones right now. Your own account will be set up for whatever you sell."
After the demo

Once they are impressed, move straight to the sign-up. Do not wait for them to think about it.

👉
Show them your QR code and ask them to scan it — or paste your referral link directly into the chat
👉
Let them know it only takes a few minutes to register and the PhiXtra team will get them set up
👉
If they are not a phone or computer shop, remind them: setup takes up to 48 hours, then they are live
