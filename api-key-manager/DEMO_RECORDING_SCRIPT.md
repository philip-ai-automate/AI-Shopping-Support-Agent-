# PhiXtra Portal — Demo Video Recording Script

**Total run time target:** 5–8 minutes  
**Demo account:** demo@phixtra.com / Demo1234!  
**Before recording:** Run `python demo_seed.py` to populate the account with data.

---

## Pre-recording checklist
- [ ] Run `python demo_seed.py` on the server
- [ ] Open an incognito browser window
- [ ] Set browser zoom to 90% (looks cleaner on screen)
- [ ] Hide bookmarks bar
- [ ] Close notifications / set to Do Not Disturb
- [ ] Open portal.phixtra.com/login and stop there

---

## SCENE 1 — Login (0:00 – 0:30)

**What to show:** The clean login page.  
**Action:** Type demo@phixtra.com, type Demo1234!, click Sign In.

**Narration:**
> "Welcome to PhiXtra — the AI-powered commerce and WhatsApp platform for Nigerian businesses.
> Let me show you what the dashboard looks like for a real merchant."

---

## SCENE 2 — Dashboard (0:30 – 1:30)

**What to show:** The full dashboard — KPI cards, WhatsApp status strip, usage chart, handoff requests.

**Narration:**
> "This is the merchant dashboard. At a glance you can see your WhatsApp connection status,
> how many AI conversations happened today and this month, your active leads, and any customers
> waiting for a human agent.
>
> The chart shows 30 days of AI activity. Today alone there have been [X] conversations handled
> entirely by the AI, with no human involvement needed.
>
> These red cards here — these are customers who asked to speak to a human agent.
> Let's come back to those."

**Tip:** Hover over the chart bars to show the tooltip.

---

## SCENE 3 — WhatsApp Conversations / Inbox (1:30 – 2:30)

**Action:** Click **Conversations** in the sidebar.

**What to show:** The inbox listing — 8 contacts, last message preview, lead score pills.

**Narration:**
> "Every WhatsApp conversation your AI has with a customer is captured here.
> You can see who's hot, who's warm — these are scored automatically based on
> buying intent signals in their messages.
>
> Let me open one."

**Action:** Click on **Chukwuemeka Obi** (or any contact with a long conversation).

**What to show:** The full message thread — AI greeting, product questions, AI responses, ORDER message.

**Narration:**
> "Here's the full conversation. The AI greeted the customer, found out their name,
> answered product questions — iPhone 15 specs, pricing in Naira — and guided them
> to place an order. All automated, 24/7, even at 2am.
>
> Notice the AI never invents prices or products — it only pulls from your product knowledge base."

---

## SCENE 4 — Contacts (2:30 – 2:50)

**Action:** Click **Contacts** in the sidebar.

**What to show:** The contacts list with names and phone numbers.

**Narration:**
> "Every customer who contacts you is automatically saved as a contact.
> You can add notes, and they sync with your conversation history."

---

## SCENE 5 — AI Assistant Settings (2:50 – 3:40)

**Action:** Click **AI Assistant** → **System Instruction** in the sidebar.

**What to show:** The system prompt editor with the full customised prompt.

**Narration:**
> "This is where you customise how your AI assistant behaves.
> You can set the greeting, define pricing rules, set the language — including Nigerian Pidgin —
> and control exactly what the AI can and cannot say.
>
> This is your AI's personality. You own it completely."

**Action:** Click **AI Assistant** → **Handoff Rules**.

**What to show:** The 5 handoff rules — keyword triggers, time limit.

**Narration:**
> "Handoff rules decide when the AI steps aside and alerts you.
> For example — if a customer says 'speak to a human', the AI immediately flags it
> and you get notified to take over. You can add any keywords, and set a time limit too."

## SCENE 6 — WhatsApp Setup (3:40 – 4:10)

**What to show:** The connected WhatsApp number panel — green status, phone number, verified name.

## Recording tips

- Record at 1920×1080, 30fps
- Use Loom, OBS, or QuickTime
- Speak slowly — you can speed up in editing but not slow down
- Pause 1 second between sections before moving the mouse
- Don't rush the chart animations — let them render
- Add a background music track at 10–15% volume in post
